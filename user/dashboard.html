About Pizza Palace

Our Story

Founded in 2025, SK's Pizza started as a small family-owned pizzeria with a passion for authentic Filipino flavors. What began as a humble neighborhood spot has grown into one of the most beloved pizza destinations in the town.

We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients, from our homemade dough to our locally sourced vegetables and premium meats. Our secret family recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring every bite is packed with flavor.

At SK's Pizza, we believe pizza is more than just food - it's a way to bring people together, create memories, and share joy. That's why we put so much love into every pizza we make.
